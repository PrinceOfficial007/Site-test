Our Story


Mopas Mining (GH) Limited is a Limited Liabilty Company based in Ghana and it was established in August, 2012 with the main purpose to provide precious metals and comprehensive logistics services to customers all over the world.


Unearthing Ghana's golden potential, Mopas Mining delves deep into the heart of Abooso, Begoro. This gold mining company leverages expertise and innovation to extract and refine precious metals, contributing to the nation's economic prosperity.


We are focused on acquisition, exploration and sourcing of Gold in an evironmentally safe method and effectively controling pollution on our mining sites and environments with the objective of providing value added services for our esteemed customers and stakeholders.


Our Company is duly registerd under the Laws of the Republic of Ghana and we have the appropraite certifications to export Gold without restrictions to any part of the world.


We are proud to announce that we have crossed over $100 million dollars worth of precious metals export and we frequently export Gold to Europe, Asia, and USA.
